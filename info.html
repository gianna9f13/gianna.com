Spongebob SquarePants is a popular American animated series created by Stephen Hillenburg. The show premiered on July 17,1999. The series is about the quirky adventures of Spongebob and his underwater friends in the fictional city of Bikini Bottom. Spongebob is known as a cheerful and optimistic character who lives in a pineapple under the sea. Spongebob works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab. He is also known for his endless enthusiasm for jellyfishing and karate. His neighboor is also his bestfriend, Patrick.
